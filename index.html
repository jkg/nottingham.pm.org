Welcome to Nottingham.pm

Welcome to the Nottingham Perl Mongers group. We're a group of people united by a common interest in the Perl programming language, among other things. We meet around once a month, usually in a pub in Nottingham's city centre. We've risen from the ashes of a former Nottingham.pm group, now defunct — hopefully we're around for the forseeable future :-)

Get In Touch

In addition to this website, we have a mailing list kindly hosted by pm.org. You can read about it and subscribe at http://mail.pm.org/mailman/listinfo/nottingham-pm — there are also archives, visible to subscribers. Alternatively you can come find us on IRC (irc.perl.org, #nottingham.pm) or get in touch via our Twitter account, @nottinghamperl.
